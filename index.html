How does the model get these percentages?

For every possible next token, the model computes a raw compatibility score based on patterns learned from billions of examples during training — how often this kind of continuation followed similar context. A function called softmax turns those scores into probabilities that add up to 100%. The three settings below then shrink or reshape that probability list. Auto-play then randomly samples one token from whatever's left, weighted by probability — it doesn't always grab the top one, so run it a few times and watch the choice change. Clicking a token yourself just lets you try any eligible option on demand.
